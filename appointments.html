Your First Appointment

When scheduling your first appointment try to schedule at least an hour so we can call your insurance to see what you are eligible for and still have ample time for the actual fitting. We will discuss the type of surgery you have had, as well as your lifestyle and favorite activities so we can determine what breast form is best for you. You will be measured by our certified fitter who will then assist you in trying on breast forms and bras in your size to find the perfect fit and style.

What To Bring

It is a good idea to bring along a well-fitting garment so you can see how you will look in your favorite clothes.

A prescription from your physician for your garments.
